About Us

Our Profile

We are a limited liabilty business that has been suly registered with the Register General's Department, Ghana Revenue Authority, Food and Drugs Authority and the Ghana Standard Boards. We have a very serene and cozy environment that will help you relax in order to rejuvenate yourself from the stressful day activities.


Ummi's Cuisine has been in existence since 2016. We started operating as a small food joint and gradually has progressed to this level due to our committment in serving our customers with very nutritious and healthy meals under strict hygienic conditions.
