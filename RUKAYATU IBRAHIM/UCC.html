University of Cape Coast is situated in Cape Coast. the school was founded in October, 1962 with a student population of 155. Our motto is Veritas Nobis Lumen, which is of latin origin maeaning,Truth Our Guide. The colors of the school os international Klein Blue, White, Aureolin and Vermilion. It was given the nickname Cape Vars. It was established due to the then need for highly skilled and trained teachers. Its original mandate was to train graduate teachers for the second cycle institution and the Ministry of Education i order to meet the demand of teachers at that time. Though its core mandate was to train teachers, currently the school has expanded and has other faculties and different programmes and has the capacity to meet the manower neddes of other ministeries and industries inthe country.
The pionering department were the Arts and Science department. Currently the school has a student population of 74,720.The school offer regular, sandwich, distance at both the undergraduate and post graduate level.It has six traditional halls of residence

Aside the traditional Halls there are other hall of residence and other private hostels to help accomodate students and make their stay an campus very peaceful. All the halls of residence are very secured so it helps students to learn in order to attain their maximum academic heights.
